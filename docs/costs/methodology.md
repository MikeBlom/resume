# Cost-Tracking Methodology

Honest, not fake-precise.

- **AI usage** happens under a flat-rate Claude subscription, so per-session dollar figures are
  **amortized estimates**: (monthly subscription price ÷ working sessions that month), reported
  as a range. Where token counts are visible in tooling they are recorded; where they are not,
  the session count is the basis and says so.
- **Infrastructure** is listed at sticker price per provider; free tiers are recorded as $0 with
  the tier named, so readers can judge what would change at scale.
- **What is deliberately excluded:** Mike's time (the case study is about directing AI, not
  billing hours), hardware, and pre-existing subscriptions not driven by this project (except
  the amortized AI share above).
- One ledger row per work session, appended in the same PR as the session's work.
